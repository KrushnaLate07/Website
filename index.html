Correct Payment Details

Players must ensure they enter the correct payment details and transaction ID while registering. Any incorrect details may result in disqualification.

3

Payment Confirmation

CSBT E-sports Tournament Rules & Regulations

1

General Rules

  • Players must register using their correct in-game name and ID.
  • Only registered players are eligible to participate in the tournament.
  • Participants must join the lobby on time; late entries will not be entertained.
  • The use of emulators, hacks, scripts, or any third-party software is strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.
2

Match Rules

  • All matches must be played on the official BGMI app.
  • Teaming, hacking, or any form of cheating will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Intentional disconnections will not be considered for rematches.
  • If a match is interrupted due to server issues, it will be rescheduled at the discretion of the organizers.
3

Scoring & Ranking

  • The tournament will follow a points-based ranking system.
  • Final rankings will be determined based on kill points and placement points.
Special Awards:
  • MVP Award: Given to the player with the most kills in the final match.
  • Kill Leader: Awarded to the player with the highest total kills throughout the tournament.
  • Player of the Season: Given based on overall performance.
  • Tiebreaker Rule: In case of a tie, the team with more total kill points will be ranked higher.
4

Conduct & Fair Play

  • Players must maintain sportsmanship and show respect towards opponents and tournament organizers.
  • Any form of toxic behavior, abusive language, or harassment will result in a ban.
  • Any attempt to manipulate results (e.g., teaming) will lead to disqualification.
  • Players must comply with the instructions provided by tournament administrators.
  • Any disputes must be reported immediately to tournament officials along with valid evidence.
  • The decisions made by tournament officials are final and binding.

By participating in the CSBT E-sports Tournament, players agree to abide by all the above rules and regulations. Failure to comply may result in penalties, including disqualification from the tournament.
